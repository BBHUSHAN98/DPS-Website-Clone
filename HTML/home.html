Welcome to Delhi Public School, Pune

DPS Pune is one of the most renowned and trusted names in quality education, which is recognized throughout the academic world for its progressive approach and commitment to excellence. Delhi Public School, Pune was the second initiative of Takshila Educational Society. It was established in 2003 and is a collaborative effort with the Delhi Public Schools Society, which is one of the largest chains of CBSE schools in India. DPS Pune is affiliated to CBSE, New Delhi and offers education from Nursery to Class XII.

In two decades, DPS Pune has gloriously emerged to scale the pinnacle of success and is termed as one of the best K-12 institutions in western India. DPS, Pune, as a versatile multi-disciplinary institution, offers extensive facilities and imparts education par excellence. The school focuses on holistic development of students by edifying and nurturing their multiple-intelligence skills in order to make every student unique and talented.
