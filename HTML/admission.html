ADMISSION - CLASS XI

Online Registration for provisional admission to Class XI in academic session 2024-25 will commence on Friday, March 01, 2024, as per the Admission Schedule given below.

For your benefit, we have uploaded the list of subjects / streams available in our school, subject to adequate number of applicants.

The present education system is undergoing a major change with the implementation of New Education Policy (NEP-2020), National Credit Framework (NCrF-2022) and National Curriculum Framework (NCF-2023). The policies broadly emphasize that there will be no hard separation between academic or vocational streams. Hence, integration & mainstreaming of vocational education with general education is being aimed. This shall lead to re-engineering of education system. To achieve this, the board is proposing multi-disciplinarity and holistic education across Sciences, Social Sciences, Arts, Humanities, Commerce and Sports/ Vocational or Skill Subjects in Class XI & XII.

As per the recent webinar conducted by CBSE, the board plans to implement this from 2024-25 in Class XI, but it has still not come out with the final proposition.
However, the School, intends to keep you updated with the expected changes in face of implementation of the above-mentioned policies. To start with, the school may not be able to implement the changes in totality considering constraints like availability of subjects, faculty, number of class rooms etc.

At this stage, in absence of confirmation from the board, we shall follow the old system of choosing streams. However, if any change is implemented by the board in the coming months, we shall abide by the board’s directives, which may result in change of one subject in the existing stream and addition of one language native to India.

Do note that no queries will be entertained over the telephone or in person, hence please refrain from visiting or calling up the school to clarify about the provisional admission process.

For any clarification / query, you may write to admissions@dpspune.com.

  1. No change in date / time of any of the admission-related processes will be allowed. Requests for changing the date / time will not be responded to. If you cannot adhere to the schedule, please do not register.

  3. If you have not appeared for the Class X Exam as a student of our School, and are seeking admission to Class XI, refer the link for Non – DPS Pune Candidates.  The link named DPS Pune Students is only for our own Class X examinee batch.

About DPS Pune

DPS Pune is one of the most renowned and credible names in quality education. The School is recognized throughout the academic world for its progressive approach and commitment to excellence . Delhi Public School Pune, was the second initiative of Takshila Educational Society. It was established in 2003 and is a collaborative effort with the Delhi Public Schools Society, which is one of the largest chains of CBSE schools in India. DPS Pune is affiliated to CBSE, New Delhi and offers schooling from Nursery to Class XII. Within a span of two decades DPS Pune has reached the zenith of triumph and is one of the best K-12 institutions in western India. DPS, Pune, as a phenomenal multidisciplinary institution, imparts all-encompassing knowledge and facilities to keep abreast with the world. The Laser focus of the school is on the holistic development of students in order to carve them into ideal individuals.
